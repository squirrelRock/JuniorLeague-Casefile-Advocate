A JUNIOR LEAGUE OF NAPA‑SONOMA INITIATIVE PROGRAM

Since 1971, the Junior Leagues of California State Public Affairs Committee (CalSPAC) has represented the interests of its member Leagues and their community projects to policymakers at the national, state, and local levels.

CalSPAC advocates for legislation favorable to its member Leagues’ projects and issues in: education, family support, health, anti‑human trafficking, and violence prevention.

The Junior League of Napa‑Sonoma is proud to be a member of CalSPAC, and every year JLNS selects a Junior and Senior SPAC delegate to represent our League with CalSPAC.
