A JUNIOR LEAGUE OF NAPA‑SONOMA SIGNATURE PROGRAM


Our JLNS League members and guest speakers support the young women of Roseland University Prep.

We provide an interactive program on a variety of topics including self-esteem, healthy relationships, self-defense, and financial literacy.

Guest Speaker: Anamaría Morales

The final GLOW program of the 2022‑23 academic year featured Anamaría Morales, founder of College Confectionista. She graduated from UC Berkeley’s Haas School of Business as a first‑generation student, debt‑free, after growing up in a low‑income Latinx family with teenage parents. To fund her dream, she turned her baking hobby into a cheesecake fundraiser right here in Sonoma County.

The “College Confectionista”

Anamaría shared with our GLOW students how she combined entrepreneurship and baking into a thriving business. She even brought one of her signature cheesecakes for everyone to taste! One young attendee remarked, “Anamaría inspired me to think about earning for college doing what I love.”


Thank you to our community partners for supporting our ongoing program!
