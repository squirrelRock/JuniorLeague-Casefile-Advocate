GRANTS

ANNUAL GRANT APPLICATION CYCLE

Junior League of Napa-Sonoma accepts grant applications on an annual basis.  JLNS seeks to direct grants to organizations who support the League's Mission of positively impacting the lives of children and families in Sonoma and Napa Counties.

Grant applications from organizations who wish to request a grant from JLNS are usually due early in the calendar year.  This year applications were due in February 2025.

All grant applicants will be considered & notified by early May on the status of their grant application.

Grant recipients will be invited to receive their grants and be recognized at our annual celebration in May/June.

JLNS GRANTS HELP LOCAL ORGS

Our Village Closet

Grant Project 2024-25: "New Shoes for School"

OVC anticipates that over 550 youth will attend their Back to School event in need of new shoes. This initiative targets current and former foster youth aged 0-25 from Sonoma County, ensuring they receive new shoes while preserving their "freedom of choice."

Visit OVC

Redemption House

Grant Project 2024-25: "Outreach Expansion Project"

Enhance comprehensive, trauma-informed, culturally competent support services for survivors of human trafficking; increase outreach in high-trafficking areas; and boost community awareness & prevention through education and advocacy training programs.

Visit Redemption House

Children's Museum of Sonoma Co

Grant Project 2024-2025: "MOG Homeless/At Risk of Being Homeless Project"

Museum-on-the-Go (MOG) will strategically prioritize children who are homeless or at risk, while also serving the Latinx/migrant worker community & local Native American tribes.

Visit the MOG
