MUSEUM ON THE GO

A JUNIOR LEAGUE OF NAPA‑SONOMA INITIATIVE PROGRAM

Delivering Transformative Experiences to our Community

In 2008, The Junior League of Napa-Sonoma provided the first seed funding to officially sponsor the Museum-On-The-Go, expanding outreach across the county.

Museum-On-The-Go participates in off-site community family events in order to bring our unique programming to children who may not otherwise be able to visit our permanent location.

Supported through JLNS with our Member and Community Donations

Museum-On-The-Go features collaborative STEAM-based activities such as build-you-own rockets and nature-based creative arts.

Museum-On-The-Go explores physics concepts with construction projects, Keva planks, and Imagination Playground blocks. Activities are designed for children ages 3-10.
