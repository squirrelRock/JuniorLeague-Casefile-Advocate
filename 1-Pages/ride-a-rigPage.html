JUNIOR LEAGUE OF NAPA-SONOMA SIGNATURE FUNDRAISER: RIDE-A-RIG

Ride-a-Rig is a community event where children can see and touch a variety of cool vehicles.
Come explore the fire trucks, public safety vehicles, big rigs, race cars, and much, much more!
We open at 10AM siren-free for anyone with sensory sensitivities.
Sirens and "Beep Beeps" will sound from 1-2 PM.

Discover Sonoma County resources...

  • Meet first responders who play important roles in protecting our community
  • Learn about organizations working to improve local resources
  • Find local businesses that interest you

All ages are welcome!

Plus... come explore these activities:

  • Bounce your heart out in our massive jumpy house!
  • Children's Museum of Sonoma County: Museum-on-the-Go
  • Check out books from the Sonoma County Library's Bibliobus
  • Visit with Clo the Cow and taste delicious treats
  • Oliver's pretend play store & market
  • Photo-op with Storm Troopers
  • Fill your water bottles at the City of Santa Rosa hydration station
  • Honk a Horn or Trigger a Siren (1-2pm only)
