Membership Eligibility

A prospective member of JLNS shall have an interest in voluntarism, a commitment to community service, and a desire to develop the skills and abilities that will better enable her to serve the community.

A prospective member shall be 21 years of age or older. There is no upper age limit.

Junior League of Napa-Sonoma uses only the above criteria for consideration of prospective members.

We highly recommend that potential new members get to know JLNS by attending three JLNS activities before requesting a membership application: an informational meeting; at least one general membership meeting; and one social or volunteer event.

JLNS Member Meeting Requirements

All JLNS members shall:

Attend a minimum of 4 out of 8 JLNS Monthly General Membership Meetings. These are held on the fourth Wednesday evening of each month and may be attended either in-person or virtually.

A member must have schedule compatibility with at least four, and preferably more, Wednesday night meetings during each year.

Attend New Member Informational Meetings & new member classes

Complete a New Member community project

Community Service / Council Requirements

Serve on a council & attend all council mettings (monthly -virtually or in-person)

Participate in volunteering opportunties as your scheduling allows

Fulfill any fundraising requirements voted on by the general membership.
These requirements usually include: volunteering a certain number of hours at a fund-raising event; participating in set-up or take-down; selling tickets; and/or collecting financial sponsorships.
Check out our annual Ride-a-Rig event as an example to see how you can help.
