OUR MISSION

We are an organization of women whose mission is to advance women's leadership for meaningful community impact through volunteer action, collaboration, and training. Our purpose is exclusively educational and charitable.

OUR VISION

We are an active and diverse group of trained volunteers who are committed to positively impacting the lives of children and families. We join our efforts with over 290 Leagues in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and England through membership in the Association of Junior Leagues International, Inc.

OUR VALUES

The Junior League welcomes all women who value our Mission. We are committed to inclusive environments of diverse individuals, organizations, and communities.
